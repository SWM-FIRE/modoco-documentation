One thing Zoom can’t do is allow for students to chat after class unless they’ve already exchanged information with each other. Discord excels in its messaging feature as students are able to chat with the class after hours, in real time and privately. -> 끝나고 채팅이 안됨 근데 이게 생각보다 중요하다
